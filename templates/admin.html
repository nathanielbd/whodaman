Rules

Guess the OUIJORDLE in six guesses with up to four of your friends.

Each player silently enters one letter in turn to form their guess. The only words players can say are YES, NO, and GOODBYE.

Players have 5 minutes to complete the OUIJORDLE, but each guess adds 30 seconds.

If a guess is not a valid word, it does not count as a guess. However, it still uses clock time.
